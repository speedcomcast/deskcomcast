Recover your Xfinity Comcast Account

  • Go to the Xfinity sign-in page and click Forgot username or password.
  • Enter your registered email address, mobile number, or username.
  • Verify your identity using the code sent to your email or phone.
  • Create a new strong password after verification.
  • Sign in again to your Xfinity Comcast account and check your account settings.
